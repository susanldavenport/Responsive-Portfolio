About Me

For the past seven years I have served as the Practice Manager of Aesthetic and Implant Dentistry of Atlanta, a cosmetic and dental implant center in North Atlanta. Recently, I became a student of Georgia Tech's Coding Bootcamp, where I am learning to be a Full Stack Developer in the hopes of beginning a successful career in Web Development.

I have been passionate about tech all my life and as a design-oriented person with extensive leadership expeirence and team-building capabilities, I hope to blend my talents and align with a company that values creativity, collaboration, and encourages a perpetual learning environment.
